Task 1:
Create a simple scraper to extract all Google reviews for the business 'BMW of Austin' from the past 1 year and save the following information to a database table.
- Date and time of the review
- Name of the reviewer
- Stars/Rating given by the reviewer
- Review text/message
Here's the google listing of the business - https://www.google.com/search?q=bmw+of+austin

Task 2:
Create a simple web app to read the saved reviews from the database and display the data on a page.

Technologies to use for the above tasks
- Python 3.x
- Beautifulsoup/Selenium (either or both)
- PostgreSQL/SQLite/MySQL (one of these)
- Flask
